Project Overview

The Personal AI Assistant is a revolutionary desktop application that brings advanced AI capabilities directly to Indonesian users' computers. Built with Tauri and integrated with local LLM models, it provides intelligent assistance while keeping all data completely private and offline.

Privacy-First Design

100% Offline Operation - No data ever leaves your computer. All AI processing happens locally, ensuring complete privacy and independence from foreign cloud services.

Development Timeline

Target: 18-24 months to stable release
Current Status: Research and architecture design phase
Dependencies: Follows completion of Aksara IS and MerdekaOS projects
